Noise Bursts Annotation Interface Instructions

You will be presented 28 recordings.

For each recording you will be asked to localize the sound you heard.

First you must play the recording.

1. To play the audio click "Play Audio".

2. If you want to pause the audio click "Pause Audio".

You may listen to the audio as many times as you want.

You can use the text box to take notes about where you heard the sound coming from. It might help you when localizing the sound.

Noise Bursts Annotation Interface Instructions

Once you’ve finished listening to the recording, you will see a 2D representation of the space, with 3 views. Please localize each of the sources identified.

The "top view" allows you to annotate azimuth or horizontal location.

The "back view" and "side view" allow you to annotate the elevation of the source.

You will see your annotation updated in real time in the 3D view to the left of your screen. You can rotate this view and zoom in or out but not annotate in it.

Noise Bursts Annotation Interface Instructions

To add a new source press "Option" or "Alt" + click.

To remove a source press "Command" or "Win" + click.

You can always see the rules in the "Keyboard Rules" button.

To create a successful annotation, you must select a horizontal position in the “top view” and an elevation position in at least one of the “back” or “side” views. Please verify the location is correct in the 3D representation to the left of your screen.
